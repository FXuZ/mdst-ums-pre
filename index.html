Performances

UMS holds regular performances every year.

Purchasing Time Pattern

The top two plots show the histogram of the relative time difference in purchase and performance date (break down into price groups). Note that it doesn't make sense to look at the relative time for subscription tickets. So we also plot the bottom plot, the purchase frequency in terms of actual (absolute) date. Each peak corresponde to a particular event, such as, the opening of the subscription series, or the opening of the single ticket sales.

Customer Dropout

We found a huge amount of buyers only make purhase in one single day in the 3 year period we are looking at. We also define the "active duration" to be the the first order date minus the last order date. We found strong peak in the 1-year period. Also we define the "Activeness score" to maintain positive and increase if someone buys in 1-month period, and decrease if someone continues to not buying. We found strong peaks in the fall and winter semester, implying people are not planning and purchasing too much ahead.
